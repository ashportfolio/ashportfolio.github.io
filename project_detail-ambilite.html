CASE STUDY


ambiLite SmartBulbs

Project Highlights

ambiLite makes LED bulbs you can control from an app on your phone. An increased use of white, modern iconography, and bolder typography gave the packaging a much-needed facelift to reach a wider audience. Inspired by packaging designs from the likes of Apple and Philips.

Redesigned

My goal here was to clean things up as always, but then also to infuse modernism and develop the brand's identity and strategy. We started by choosing to focus on mood lighting, then improved the photography, incorporated bolder typography, and modified content heavily to fit the new brand. Then, as an experiment, I redesigned it in white as an Apple-inspired nod; this turned out to be the one we loved most.
