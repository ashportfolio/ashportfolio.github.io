The Design Situation

What is the point of this unit?

Good things to know for a designer:

Why are we doing this?


Due to the increase in global warming, we would like to explore having an ‘online component’ to conservation efforts that can keep younger people engaged and educate them about a species and/or climate change’s effects. Identify a group of species / habitat(s) and create an interactive experience that will inform visitors about them and how they are impacted by global warming’s effects.

What are we wondering about?


Technology can be used as a communication platform to help humans design a better future for the environment.

Key Concept

  • Communication: how we get ideas across to one another

Global Concept

  • Fairness & Development: how we improve ourselves as individuals and a society

Related

  • Ergonomics: how humans impact the environment
  • Invention: how we make something now
  • Sustainability: how we protect the environment we live in
