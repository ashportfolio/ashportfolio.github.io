Programme Title : Bachelor of Education (Honours) (Visual Arts)
Course Title & Code : ART4217: Advanced Digital Applications
Venue : TBC
Credit Points : 3
Contact Hours : 39
Level : 4
Lecturer Email : ashwin.anandani@gmail.com

The integration of art and new media technologies enable new cultures to transform society. Technological innovation not only profoundly changes culture in the context of everyday life, it is also an essential element in the global economy and industry that advanced throughout the 21th century. To cope with the increasing demand of creative practitioners in the industry, there is a need to equip students with technical skills concerning various forms of digital media applications. This course provides students with advanced technical knowledge concerning various forms of digital media. Students will learn various techniques and related technologies through hands-on workshops of various forms of digital media production.
