CASE STUDY


Weesper.com

Project Highlights

Weesper is a talent referral platform. Remember all those times you referred a friend to a job and got nothing but a measly beer out of it? Weesper allows clients to set rewards for each hire and allows everyone to become a talent recruiter by referring people they know.

Well Designed

This one has it all: we developed a cutesy chatbot to increase sign-up conversions, limited the Dashboard to just two direct-action menu items, and then created a clean and logical process flow for referrals throughout all the user types.

Initial feedback on the usability of the Weesper platform has been extraordinarily positive since launching in September 2017, with only minor iterations to the UI as of yet.
