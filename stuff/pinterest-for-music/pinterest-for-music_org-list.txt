Concept:
‘Pinterest for Music’: collect your music into different piles for saving / listening / sharing

User Groups:
- House-lovers 25 - 44 who collect different tracks and mixes


Actions:
1 
2 
3 


Features:
- Search for vids from YouTube, SoundCloud, Beatport, and Google
- Make collections of different types of house music
- Play them as streams
- Share or recommend tracks and mixes to friends (as URL)
- Generate mixes from an artist to discover new house music
- Generate mixes from a tag to discover new house music
- Organise and add tags to music collections
- ‘Collect’ button for other sites
- Choose a bg to look at while listening —> chill.bg
